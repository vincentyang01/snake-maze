How to play

Guide your snake through the maze, eat the pellets to grow, and keep going as the speed climbs. Hitting a wall or your own tail ends the run.

Frequently asked questions

How do I steer?

Flick (swipe) in the direction you want to turn — anywhere on the screen. Prefer buttons? Turn on the on-screen D-Pad in Settings.

How do I change the colours?

Open Settings from the main menu and pick a theme: Daylight, Sunset, or Ocean.

What's the difference between Easy and Hard?

Hard moves faster and has tighter corridors. Each mode keeps its own high score, shown at the top of the main menu.

Are my high scores saved?

Yes — high scores and your settings are stored on your device.

How do I remove or manage ads?

The game is free and supported by a banner ad. You can manage ad personalisation in your device settings (reset your advertising ID). See our Privacy Policy for details.

It's not working / I found a bug.

Please email us with your device model and iOS/Android version and a short description — it really helps us fix things fast.
